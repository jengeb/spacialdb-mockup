Pricing FAQ

How am I billed?

Each database instance is billed hourly up to its monthly cost.

Am I charged when I enter a credit card?

No you are charged at the end of each month. We use your credit card to verify adequate funds.

How do I cancel my subscription?

You may cancel your subscription any time and we will only charge for the hours your database instance was alive.
